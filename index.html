Business Buddy

Business Buddy is an attractive, practical and user-friendly iOS app that has been designed to eliminate all of the pain points involved with exchanging business cards. With a strong focus on simplicity, Business Buddy has been crafted to serve a singular function using the fewest screens possible. The goal was to create an effortless user experience.
