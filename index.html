About

Hi, I’m Samantha. Nice to meet you.

I’m a passionate Ottawa based designer crafting products and experiences that inform, inspire and captivate the viewer. I'm thrilled by the idea of giving life to creativity and I want to share this passion with others by helping to bring their ideas to life.

Services

Work

Business Buddy

Business Buddy is an attractive, practical and user-friendly iOS app that has been designed to eliminate all of the pain points involved with exchanging business cards. With a strong focus on simplicity, Business Buddy has been crafted to serve a singular function using the fewest screens possible. The goal was to create an effortless user experience.

Explore TV

Explore TV is a television network with a focus on wilderness exploration. The design and branding aspects of the network were created to visually represent the sense of adventure that goes along with the television shows provided by this network. The completed identity was brought forth into a unified broadcast package that includes the network’s opening title, lower thirds and transitional elements, all of which would occur on this channel.

Woodhouse Brewing Co.

The Woodhouse Brewing Company is an honest, independently owned brewery founded in 2014. The company’s identity is heavily influenced by a loving community of family, friends, and beer lovers. The completed print work and identity for the brewery was designed to visually represent the sense of neighbourly familiarity and trust that the family-named brewing company stands for. The inspired type, form and colour scheme sets the Woodhouse Brewing Company apart from it’s competition and into the hands of their desired audience of friendly beer lovers.

Sportcheck UI Kit

Designed specifically for mobile, this UI Kit was crafted to compliment the experience of ordering a product from the Sportcheck website on a mobile device. In order to benefit the user, elements of the site were redesigned to be more iconic and simplistic and the process of placing an order was made easier. The kit was designed to improve Sportcheck’s user experience on a mobile device, but was also designed to stay true to their strong and unique brand identity.

Langly

Langly Bag Co. provides fashionable and functional camera bags and accessories to photographers all around the world. Inspired by the life of the freelance photographer, this promotional video was designed to showcase the same sense of adventure that the company's target audience knows all too well.

SAS Survival Guide

SAS Survival Guide is an application that brings the user a definitive guide of elite training techniques used to survive in the wild. The redesigned icons can be described as a modern interpretation of their previous icons, simplified to better convey their messages. Each icon was crafted to be part of a visually unified group that would benefit the user’s experience by providing them with a cleaner and clearer design.

Toronto Wildlife Centre

The Toronto Wildlife Centre is a registered charity (supported primarily by donations) that strives to help people and wildlife coexist peacefully. The completed logo and identity work for the Toronto Wildlife Centre has been designed to give the company a more modern and simplistic brand that better represents what they strive so hard to achieve. The symbol was created to take on both horizontal and vertical layouts, while maintaining a simplistic beauty that can be reproduced at any reasonable size.
